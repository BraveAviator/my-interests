The Characters from The Lord of the Rings and The Hobbit

Characters From The Hobbit

Bilbo Baggins: A friendly Hobbit from the Shire. His family is pretty rich considering most it came from his mother. He is a mix of a Took and Baggins, which are two common families in Hobbiton. Tooks like to go on adventures while Baggins on the other hand don't. Bilbo has these personality toggles often and is the overall main chacter of "The Hobbit"

Gandalf: The guy who brings Bilbo Baggins onto the adventure. Bilbo originally did not want to go on the adventure so Gandalf kind of forced him to the adventure.

Thorin Oakenshield: An important character in The Hobbit as Bilbo would not have had an adventure to go on without him. He is the leader of the adventure known as "The Company of Thorin Oakenshield".

Beorn: Another important character as he is usually no fond of dwarves. But he hates orcs more than anything else and would help the dwarves to stay away and be safer from orcs.

Gollum: This is an interesting character that shows up at Chapter Five of The Hobbit. He is the current ring-bearer of the One Ring to rule them all. This ring leads into "The Lord of the Rings".

Bard: He helps with various things including giving the dwarves things they need to complete their journey after Thorin gives them a promise.

Smaug: The dragon is an important part of this story because without the dragon there also would not be an adventure. He has stolen the dwarves home of Erebor. The Company of Thorin Oakenshield tries to reclaim Erebor while Smaug still guards his plunder.

Characters From The Lord of the Rings

Frodo Baggins: A friendly Hobbit from the Shire. He is the nephew of the Hobbit Bilbo Baggins. He is the destined one to destroy the one ring and is the ring-bearer after Bilbo. A very important member of the Fellowship of the Ring.

Gandalf: The guy who brings Bilbo Baggins onto the adventure. Bilbo originally did not want to go on the adventure so Gandalf kind of forced him to the adventure.

Aragorn: Another important member of the Fellowship and Isildur's heir, Aragorn was a descendant of Elros Tar-Minyatur through the line of the Lords of Andúnië to Elendil, High King of Arnor and Gondor. His heritage was unknown for a long time since he was told not to tell it for a while.

Boromir: A member of the Fellowship and a man of Gondor. He is the favorite son of Denethor II along with his brother Faramir. He was a very good warrior of Gondor and had lots of good combat skills.

Gimli: Gimli is the son of Glóin and is a dwarf. He represents the race of dwarves in the Fellowship. He is the heir of the dwarves of Erebor which were very close companions with Bilbo Baggins.

Samwise Gamgee: Most people know him as the true hero of The Lord of the Rings due to his resilience and for always staying beside Frodo during dark times. There were many times where Sam did save the day as the time when Frodo was unconscious and Sam took the ring from him so it wouldn't be taken by Sauron's servants.

Meriadoc Brandybuck: Another Hobbit who was very close friends with the protagonist Frodo Baggins. He was another member of the Fellowship and has an interest of maps.

Peregrin Took: He is a Hobbit from the Shire and is mainly known for his comedy(in the films). He also did give Denethor II his service for tribute to Boromir for giving up his life and saving the Hobbits.

Legolas: He represented the elves in the Fellowship and is very close friends with Gimli. He is a silvan elf son of Thranduil.
