Apollo Mission Fun Facts!

The Apollo program was the 3rd program with human spaceflight carried my NASA. Previous programs include Mercury and Gemini.

The Apollo program was initially not planned to land on the moon but when John F Kennedy made a goal, they changed the plan of the program to land on the moon instead of shooting 3 men into space.

The first attempt of the Apollo mission ended in tradegy. Apollo 1 had a fire on February 21, 1967 killing astronauts Gus Grisom, Ed White and Roger Chaffee.

The Apollo program costed a total of $25.4 billion but it was indeed worth it making spaceflight history!

An experiment set up by Apollo 11 is still collecting data and analyzing.

In the Apollo 13 movie, Gene Kranz(Ed Harris) is known to say the famous words of "Failure is not an option"! In reality Gene never actually said this but the phrase has connected with him a lot that he made it the title of his autobiography.

The famous quote said by James Lovell in Apollo 13 movie "Houston, we have a problem" was actually said as "Houston, we've had a problem". The movie changed things a little bit but most people know the quotes from the movie.
